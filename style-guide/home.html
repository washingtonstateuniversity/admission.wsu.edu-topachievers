Apply early

Don’t miss out on our exclusive opportunities. As a high achieving freshman, apply to WSU by November 30 and you’ll receive:

  • Early consideration for admission to WSU.
  • Early consideration for elite university scholarships.
  • Access to the exclusive Future Cougars of Distinction event, specifically designed for high achieving students who qualify
  • Networking opportunities with elite WSU faculty and students.

  • You can help save the environment, like Emily Hall, a Ph.D. student in the WSU School of Biological Sciences. With the support of her WSU faculty mentors, Emily is leading her own research projects and making connections between road salts and the health of the wetlands.
  • You can win awards, like 2015 WSU graduate, Bree Berg. Bree was one of ten students nationwide selected by the American Association for Cancer Research to receive the Thomas F. Bardos Science Research Education Award. It was WSU that helped her get her research into prostate cancer on the map.
  • You can dream big, like WSU Ph.D. student Ian Richardson. Ian wants to replace fossil fuels with hydrogen, he wants to design rockets, and he wants to travel to space. And, with the help of the WSU Hydrogen Properties for Energy Research Lab, Ian is making him dreams come true.
